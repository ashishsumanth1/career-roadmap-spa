Phase 2: Build Your Portfolio

A strong portfolio is your most critical asset. It's tangible proof of your skills. Aim for 2-3 high-quality, end-to-end projects that demonstrate a range of abilities and link technical work to business value.

Phase 3: Brand & Network

With new skills and projects, it's time to build your professional brand. A modern resume, an optimized LinkedIn profile, and active networking are key to uncovering opportunities in the hidden job market.

Phase 4: Interview & Secure

Success in data science interviews comes from preparation. Be ready to tackle technical questions, articulate your experiences using the STAR method for behavioural questions, and think structurally through business case studies.
